HSN Classification using AI

Introduction to HSN

The Harmonized System Nomenclature (HSN) is an internationally standardized system of names and numbers for classifying traded products. It is used by customs authorities around the world to identify products when assessing duties and taxes and for gathering statistics.

Importance of Images and Text in HSN Classification

Images and text descriptions play a crucial role in accurate HSN classification:

  • Images provide visual cues about the product's appearance, materials, and features.
  • Text descriptions offer detailed information about the product's composition, use, and characteristics.
  • Combining both image and text data allows for more accurate and robust classification.

Technologies Used

  • Image-to-Text: Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) for feature extraction from images
  • Text-to-Text Transfer Learning: BERT-based models fine-tuned on product descriptions
  • Deep Learning: Multi-modal fusion of image and text features for final classification
  • Training Data: Over 13 million labeled samples for robust model performance
