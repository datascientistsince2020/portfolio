About Botified

Botified is a sophisticated tool that utilizes advanced machine learning algorithms to monitor and enhance the performance of chatbots. By providing in-depth insights into customer experience and satisfaction, Botified empowers organizations to optimize their chatbot interactions, ultimately leading to improved customer retention and operational efficiency. It is impactful and used by some B2C Fortune 500 companies in Finance, Healthcare and E-commerce.
